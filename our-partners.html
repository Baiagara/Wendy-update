Our Trusted Partners

We are proud to collaborate with these incredible organizations and companies that help us make a real impact.

Jewish National Fund USA

Jewish National Fund USA supports the development of Israel's land, water, and infrastructure and promotes Jewish unity and philanthropy.

Magen David Adom

Magen David Adom is Israel's national emergency medical service, providing ambulance and blood bank services.

Jewish Federation of Greater Houston

The Jewish Federation of Greater Houston enhances Jewish life and supports social services in the Houston community and Israel.

Jewish Federation of Greater Philadelphia

The Jewish Federation of Greater Philadelphia supports local and global Jewish needs through community-building and philanthropy.

Jewish Agency for Israel

The Jewish Agency for Israel strengthens Jewish identity and supports the Jewish people through aliyah, education, and community services.

Israel Defense Forces (IDF)

The IDF collaborates with Sar-El to support logistical and humanitarian efforts, ensuring security and assistance during times of need.

World Zionist Organization

The World Zionist Organization promotes Jewish settlement and community development in Israel and supports global Jewish unity.

IsraAID

IsraAID provides emergency relief and development aid to vulnerable populations in crisis situations around the world.

Yad Vashem

Yad Vashem is dedicated to preserving the memory of the Holocaust and promoting Holocaust education and remembrance.

Hadassah Medical Organization

Hadassah Medical Organization provides high-quality healthcare services and medical research, contributing to health improvements in Israel.

HIAS

HIAS provides protection and humanitarian aid to refugees and displaced people, supporting their integration and resettlement.

United Israel Appeal

United Israel Appeal supports humanitarian and development projects in Israel, enhancing the lives of its residents through various initiatives.

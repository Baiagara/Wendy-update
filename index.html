About Us

The purpose of Sar-El is to enable supporters from around the world, and at home, to contribute to the security of the State of Israel by assisting on IDF bases in a logistical support role. By volunteering, participants not only help with essential tasks but also gain a deeper understanding of the challenges faced by Israel and its defense forces.

Sar-El Israel is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing humanitarian aid and support to communities affected by the Israel-Hamas conflict. Our mission is to promote peace, understanding, and cooperation through volunteer efforts and community engagement. We strive to foster a spirit of solidarity and mutual aid among people from diverse backgrounds, united by a common goal of supporting Israel.

Sar-El's vision extends beyond immediate logistical support. We aim to build a global network of informed and engaged individuals who advocate for Israel's security and contribute to its societal resilience. By promoting volunteerism, we hope to inspire future generations to uphold the values of service, dedication, and international cooperation.

Read Before You Come

Before embarking on your volunteer journey with Sarel Israel, please take note of the following important information:

  • Ensure you have a valid passport with at least six months validity beyond your planned stay
  • Check visa requirements based on your nationality
  • Familiarize yourself with local customs and cultural norms
  • Pack appropriate clothing for both work and leisure
  • Bring any necessary medications and a basic first-aid kit
  • Be prepared for potentially challenging emotional and physical conditions

Areas Of Activity

Connect with Soldiers

Engage with soldiers and provide moral support during their service. Your interaction can make a significant difference in their lives, offering them encouragement and a sense of community.

Assist the IDF

Help in logistical and administrative tasks that support the Israel Defense Forces. Your contributions ensure that resources are efficiently distributed, aiding the overall mission.

Experience Israel

Immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of Israel. Participate in guided tours, educational programs, and cultural exchanges to deepen your understanding and appreciation of the region.

Contribute Today

Your donations directly support various initiatives aimed at improving the lives of those in need. From humanitarian aid to educational programs, your contribution makes a real impact.

Program Schedule

Our volunteer programs run throughout the year. Here's a general outline of our schedule:

  • Spring Program: March 1 - May 31
  • Summer Program: June 1 - August 31
  • Fall Program: September 1 - November 30
  • Winter Program: December 1 - February 28

Each program includes orientation, training, on-site work, and cultural exchange activities. Specific schedules will be provided upon acceptance into the program.

Celebrating 40 Years of Sar-El

Join us in celebrating 40 years of Sar-El and honoring General Aharon David for his invaluable contributions to our organization and its mission. Over the past four decades, Sar-El has:

  • Mobilized over 250,000 volunteers from 60 countries
  • Contributed millions of work hours to support Israel
  • Fostered lasting friendships and cultural understanding
  • Provided crucial support during times of conflict and peace

We invite you to be part of our continuing legacy of service and solidarity.
